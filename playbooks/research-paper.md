# Research Paper Playbook

## Purpose

Use this to read and extract value from papers.

## Process

1. Read title, abstract, figures, and conclusion.
2. Identify main claim.
3. Identify problem.
4. Understand method.
5. Check experiments.
6. Check baselines.
7. Identify limitations.
8. Extract useful ideas.
9. Decide whether it matters for current projects.

## Output

```markdown
## One-line summary
## Problem
## Method
## Results
## Limitations
## Practical applications
## Open questions
```
