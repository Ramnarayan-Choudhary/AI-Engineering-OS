# DECISIONS.md

A running record of decisions that a future session (human or AI) would otherwise re-litigate. The reasoning is the valuable part — capture *why*, not just *what*.

Add newest entries at the top.

## Entry template

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD — <decision title>

Decision:
What was decided.

Context:
The situation and constraints that forced a choice.

Alternatives:
What else was considered.

Reasoning:
Why this option won.

Tradeoffs:
What we gave up by choosing it.

Revisit when:
The condition under which this should be reconsidered.
```
