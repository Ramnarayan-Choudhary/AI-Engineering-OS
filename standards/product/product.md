# Product Standards

## Principle

Start with users and problems, not technology.

## Product discovery questions

- Who is the user?
- What painful problem do they have?
- How do they solve it today?
- Why is the current solution insufficient?
- What is the smallest useful solution?
- How will success be measured?

## Good MVP

A good MVP is:

- Small
- Useful
- Testable
- Easy to explain
- Focused on one clear pain point

## AI behavior

DO:

- Push toward user value.
- Remove unnecessary features.
- Ask what problem is being solved.

DON'T:

- Start from technology alone.
- Add features because they are interesting.
