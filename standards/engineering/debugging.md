# Debugging Standards

## Debugging process

```text
Observe symptoms
↓
Reproduce
↓
Collect evidence
↓
Form hypotheses
↓
Test one hypothesis at a time
↓
Find root cause
↓
Fix minimally
↓
Verify
↓
Record lesson if recurring
```

## Rules

- Do not guess.
- Do not change many things at once.
- Do not patch symptoms without understanding root cause.
- Prefer minimal reproductions.
- Use logs, tests, traces, and diffs.
