# Research Standards

## Purpose

Use this for research discussions, paper reading, experiment planning, and idea development.

## Research thinking process

1. Define problem.
2. Explain why it matters.
3. Identify prior work.
4. Identify gap.
5. State hypothesis.
6. Propose method.
7. Define evaluation.
8. Choose baselines.
9. Discuss limitations.
10. Connect to application.

## AI behavior

DO:

- Explain intuition first.
- Compare with existing work.
- Discuss limitations.
- Suggest experiments.
- Be careful about novelty claims.

DON'T:

- Oversell ideas.
- Ignore baselines.
- Confuse implementation difficulty with research contribution.
